Our Work

Our Artists

Sophia Müller

Sophia is a master of black & grey realism, bringing lifelike portraits, skulls, and religious imagery to life with smooth shading and depth. Her whip shading technique creates soft gradients, making her tattoos look like they are straight out of a photograph. Whether it’s a highly detailed face, an intricate animal design, or a gothic masterpiece, Sophia's precision and artistic eye make her one of the best in the business.

Diego Navarro

Diego is known for his bold, neo-traditional designs and Japanese-inspired tattoos. He combines thick outlines, vibrant colors, and detailed shading to create powerful and expressive designs. From fierce dragons and koi fish to classic neo-traditional roses and skulls, Diego’s work is a fusion of tradition and modern artistry. His tattoos have a strong visual impact and a timeless appeal.

Eva Laurent

Eva specializes in fine line tattoos, creating elegant and delicate designs with razor-thin precision. She is highly skilled in minimalist tattoos, single-needle techniques, and micro tattoos. Her work is perfect for those who want subtle yet meaningful pieces, such as small floral designs, minimalist portraits, and abstract linework.

Freya Andersen

Freya's tattoos are bold, dark, and intense. Specializing in blackwork and dark art, she creates intricate, high-contrast tattoos with heavy black shading and strong negative space. Her style is inspired by gothic imagery, occult symbols, and surrealist horror, making her work ideal for those who love edgy and dramatic tattoos.
