About Us

We are a crew of very passionate people from all over the world, we speak five different languages, who loves to create a lifetime worth of art and memories. Our shop has five permanent artist, one apprentice, and one shop manager that is happy to talk to everyone that is interested in what we do but also if you have any specific questions about the procedure, the design or just how bad it hurts! We are also proud to have a big range of guest artist regularly visiting us. So whatever style you’re looking for, we have it! If you want to see more of what we do in the shop, tattoos, designs, drawings, general fun stuff that happens we recommend to visit our Instagram @lucky13ink and give us a follow. From Tuesdays-Saturdays we update you guys with upcoming events, guest-artist that will visit and all the work we do. You also get a view of all the artists and a look of how the studio is in live acton!

Our Artists

Sophia Müller

Sophia is a master of black & grey realism, bringing lifelike portraits, skulls, and religious imagery to life with smooth shading and depth.

Diego Navarro

Diego is known for his bold, neo-traditional designs and Japanese-inspired tattoos. He combines thick outlines, vibrant colors, and detailed shading.

Eva Laurent

Eva specializes in fine line tattoos, creating elegant and delicate designs with razor-thin precision. She is highly skilled in minimalist tattoos.
